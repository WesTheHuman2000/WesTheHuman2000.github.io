Project overview

  • Describe your application and its purpose
      • My application is a webpage that is made to teach and show people woodgas stoves. The site is also to teach people how to make wood gas stoves
  • Who are the intended users of the website?
      • Intended audience of the site would be people that do not know much about woodgas stoves and might be interested in them.
  • Overview of the content of the website
      • I intend to have a home page, a page with materials and how to build these stoves, a page showcasing different stoves, a page to calculate costs of building your own stove, and a page with some things that you could cook with a wood gas stove.
